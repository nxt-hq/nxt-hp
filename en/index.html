Frequently asked

Do we need a Japanese subsidiary or local staff?

No. We act as your importer and distributor in Japan. You sell to one customer — us — and we handle import, sales, marketing and support locally.

What does the partnership cost?

Nothing. There are no setup fees, retainers or marketing charges. We earn a distribution margin when your product sells — so we only win when you do.

What kinds of products are you looking for?

Quality consumer products for home, wellness and workspace — things that make everyday life better. If your product has a genuine story and craftsmanship behind it, we want to hear about it, even outside these categories.

How large is the first order?

It depends on the product and price point. We typically start with a trial purchase order that is meaningful enough to properly test the market, then scale with demand.

Can we communicate in English?

Yes. We work with our German partner in English every week. Contracts, reporting and day-to-day communication in English are no problem.
